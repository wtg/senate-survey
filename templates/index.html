Presidential Selection Committee Student Representative Application

Summer 2021

We are requesting applicants for two student representative positions on the Presidential Search Committee. These students would work alongside members of the Board of Trustees, alumni, and faculty to determine the future of Rensselaer. Responsibilities include researching and interviewing potential candidates, while voicing the concerns and interests of the current student body. We are looking for someone engaged in campus life, with the capacity for self-reflection and long-term thinking, to represent the student body on this committee.

You may only complete this application once, so please review your answers prior to submission. Your answers are not saved upon leaving the page. Applications will close on Friday, July 23rd.

Apply Now

Questions for Review

If you would like to review the long response questions before applying, they are listed below.

  • What clubs and extracurricular activities are you involved in on campus?
  • What does the future of RPI look like to you?
  • What traits are most important in a presidential candidate?
  • What perspectives would you bring to the presidential selection committee?
